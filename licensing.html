Licensing, Ownership, and Credits

Editorial transparency page

This page explains how Brush Jjaemu Space distinguishes between its own original site materials and third-party games, brands, or host pages referenced on the site. The main goal is clarity: visitors should be able to understand what belongs to this site and what remains the property of original creators or publishers.

What belongs to Brush Jjaemu Space

Unless stated otherwise, Brush Jjaemu Space claims ownership only over its original site presentation, front-end styling, editorial writing, guide articles, policy pages, informational copy, and site-specific branding elements created for this project.

What does not belong to this site

Third-party game code, source pages, trademarks, logos, trade dress, in-game art, audio, characters, and related intellectual property remain the property of their respective owners, developers, or publishers. Mentioning a title, linking to a source host, or presenting a game-related page on Brush Jjaemu Space does not transfer or imply ownership of those third-party materials.

Brush Jjaemu credit

Brush Jjaemu Space identifies Brush Jjaemu as the featured game on the site and credits the original creator where that information is available. When a page references a different host for playable access, that host is described separately so authorship and hosting are not treated as the same thing.

Third-party mini games and source hosts

Some pages on the site may reference, link to, or organize third-party browser-game hosts as part of a broader game-library structure. Those pages are intended to help visitors navigate the site and understand where a source host is involved, but they do not grant reuse rights to underlying third-party content. If a rightsholder wants a page reviewed, corrected, or removed, the site encourages direct contact.

Requests for correction, credit, or removal

If you are a creator, publisher, or rights representative and believe a page needs updated attribution, corrected ownership information, or removal, email hello@brushjjaemu.space. If the issue involves copyright infringement allegations, use the more detailed process on the DMCA page.

No implied license

Nothing on Brush Jjaemu Space should be read as granting permission to copy, redistribute, republish, or commercially exploit third-party games or brands. Any permission to reuse third-party materials must come from the original rightsholder, not from this site.
