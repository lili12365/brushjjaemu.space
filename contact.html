Contact

Contact Brush Jjaemu Space

Primary contact: hello@brushjjaemu.space

Brush Jjaemu Space uses email as its main contact channel. If you need to report a technical problem, ask a question about the site's editorial pages, request a correction, or raise a rights issue, use the contact details below and include enough detail for the page or problem to be identified quickly.

General and editorial inquiries

For editorial feedback, content suggestions, page corrections, or general questions about Brush Jjaemu Space, email hello@brushjjaemu.space. If you are writing about a specific URL, include the page link and a short explanation of what you want reviewed.

Technical issues

If a page is loading poorly, a link is broken, a game frame fails to open, or a browser-specific problem is affecting the site, include the page URL, browser name, device type, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots and reproduction steps are useful when available.

Licensing, attribution, and rights questions

If you are a creator, publisher, or rightsholder and want to discuss credit, ownership labels, removal, corrections, or licensing concerns, use the same email address and state clearly that your request is rights related. For copyright notices and takedown requests, the dedicated process is described on the DMCA page.

What to include in your message

  • Your name and a working contact email address.
  • The exact page URL or game title involved.
  • A short explanation of the issue or request.
  • Any evidence, correction details, or supporting links that help verify the request.

Response expectations

Brush Jjaemu Space is a small editorial project, so response times may vary. Priority is generally given to copyright, licensing, abuse, and factual-correction requests before routine feedback or non-urgent suggestions.
